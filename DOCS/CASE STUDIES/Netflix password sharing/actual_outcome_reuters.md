## Reuters

Reuters is a global news agency founded in London in 1851 by German-born entrepreneur Paul Julius Reuter. It is now the news and media division of Thomson Reuters and one of the largest international multimedia news providers, renowned for accuracy, speed, and adherence to its Trust Principles of integrity, independence, and freedom from bias.

### Key facts

- **Founded:** 1851, London, United Kingdom
- **Founder:** Paul Julius Reuter
- **Headquarters:** London, England (with major hubs in New York, Toronto, Singapore)
- **Parent company:** Thomson Reuters (since 2008)
- **Employees:** ~2,600 journalists in 165 countries
- **Languages:** 12+

### Origins and growth

Reuters began as a telegraph-based service transmitting stock prices and news between European cities. Early use of carrier pigeons and electric telegraphy gave the firm a reputation for unmatched speed. By the late 19th century it had become Britain’s leading news wire, expanding globally through submarine cables and correspondent networks.

### Trust Principles and governance

In 1941, to safeguard editorial independence, Reuters adopted its **Trust Principles**, mandating impartiality, integrity, and freedom from control by any faction. These principles remain embedded in Thomson Reuters’s constitutional documents, overseen by independent directors to prevent political or commercial influence.

### Digital transformation

Reuters pioneered electronic distribution of financial data in the 1960s and launched computer-terminal displays for foreign-exchange rates in 1973. It entered the electronic trading and data services market in the 1980s, later becoming a key source of real-time financial and general news. Following its 2008 merger with The Thomson Corporation, Reuters retained its editorial identity within the new conglomerate, Thomson Reuters.

### Scope and operations

The agency delivers over two million unique stories, 1.5 million photos, and 130,000 videos annually. It supplies media outlets, governments, corporations, and technology firms with breaking news, photography, video, graphics, and data journalism. Reuters Connect and API systems allow seamless content distribution to clients worldwide.

### Recognition and impact

Reuters journalists have earned multiple Pulitzer Prizes and George Polk Awards for national reporting, business coverage, and photography. Its blend of speed, accuracy, and global reach continues to make Reuters a cornerstone of modern journalism and a standard-bearer for impartial news dissemination.
