# MAIN

## Objective

Simulate the global ripple effects of the current U.S.-Iran war (Operation Epic Fury), focusing on the intersection of the Strait of Hormuz closure, the death of Ali Khamenei, and the resulting volatility in global energy markets and AI infrastructure security over the next 30 days.

## Insight

Analyze the 'Feedback Loop' between military escalation and economic shift: How does the Iranian demand for oil payments in Chinese Yuan/Crypto, combined with the U.S. targeting of Iranian infrastructure, impact the long-term stability of the 'Petrodollar' and the security of global Big Tech assets in the Gulf?

## What if Scenario

The U.S. successfully re-opens the Strait of Hormuz within 72 hours using autonomous naval swarms, but Iran successfully executes a 'Hard Reset' cyberattack on the Saudi Aramco data centers.

The Goal: See if the 63% "Erosion" group switches focus to the Security of AI assets once the energy crisis is "solved."
